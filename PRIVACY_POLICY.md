Privacy Policy for JyotiGPT
Last updated: 1/20/2026

JyotiGPT respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use JyotiGPT, an independently managed, publicly available AI-powered application.

1. About JyotiGPT
JyotiGPT is an AI-based application designed to provide spiritual reflections, general guidance, and Murli-inspired insights.

Important Disclaimer:
JyotiGPT is developed independently by a member of the Brahma Kumaris community. While certain Brahma Kumaris centres may use or share it as a resource for educational and inspirational purposes, JyotiGPT remains a separate initiative and may occasionally be financially sponsored by the Brahma Kumaris World Spiritual University or its centers.

JyotiGPT does not generate profit.
JyotiGPT is not a substitute for personal spiritual study, meditation, or guidance from qualified teachers.

2. Information We Collect
a. Information You Provide
Text inputs such as questions, reflections, or prompts
Feedback voluntarily submitted
b. Automatically Collected Information
Device and browser type
Approximate usage data (non-identifying)
JyotiGPT does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personally identifiable information.

3. How Your Information Is Used
To generate AI responses
To maintain security and prevent misuse
To improve reliability and performance
JyotiGPT does not sell personal data, display targeted advertising, or share user data for commercial purposes.

4. AI Processing & Third-Party Services
JyotiGPT uses third-party AI infrastructure to generate responses. User inputs may be processed temporarily by these services solely for response generation.

Conversations are not intentionally stored long-term and are not used to train AI models unless explicitly stated.

5. Data Storage & Retention
Any data retained is stored only as long as necessary for operational, safety, or improvement purposes and is deleted or anonymized thereafter.

6. Spiritual Content Disclaimer
JyotiGPT provides spiritual and reflective content for general guidance only. Users should avoid sharing sensitive personal, medical, legal, or financial information.

7. Children's Privacy
JyotiGPT is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

8. GDPR Rights (EEA & UK Users)
If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

The right to access your data
The right to request correction or deletion
The right to restrict or object to processing
The right to data portability, where applicable
Since JyotiGPT does not maintain user accounts or permanent personal profiles, data requests will be handled on a best-effort basis.

9. CCPA Rights (California Residents)
Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to:

Know what personal data is collected
Request deletion of personal data
Opt out of the sale of personal data
JyotiGPT does not sell personal data and does not discriminate against users for exercising privacy rights.

10. Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
